Reginald "Rex" Peak, CFP, is a dedicated financial advisor specializing in the unique needs of High-Altitude Estates. Rex is committed to building long-term relationships based on trust and a deep understanding of his clients' financial goals. Beyond the office, Rex's dedication and perseverance extend to the mountains; he has summitted every 14er in the state of Colorado.
