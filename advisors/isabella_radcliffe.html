Benjamin "Ben" Spud is a dedicated financial advisor specializing in agribusiness risk management. Grounded in a deep understanding of the agricultural sector, Ben leverages his expertise to help farmers and agribusiness owners navigate the unique financial challenges of this vital industry. He earned his degree from the University of Idaho, building a strong foundation for his advisory work. Ben is an accredited Agribusiness and Farm Insurance Specialist (AFIS), demonstrating his commitment to providing specialized and insightful solutions. With a client-focused approach, he strives to build long-term relationships based on trust and tailored strategies. Outside of the office, Ben indulges in his passion for agricultural history, maintaining a collection of rare 19th-century farm tools.
