Crescent Cityman, CPWA, is a dedicated financial advisor specializing in intergenerational wealth strategies. With a deep understanding of the complexities involved in transferring and preserving assets across generations, Crescent helps families build lasting legacies. He guides clients through intricate financial planning, estate optimization, and investment strategies designed to benefit future generations. Crescent’s approach is rooted in a personalized understanding of each family’s unique goals and values. He earned his degree from Tulane University, providing him with a strong foundation in finance and a commitment to serving his community. Beyond finance, Crescent is a classically trained jazz trumpeter, bringing a unique blend of precision and creativity to his client relationships.
