Ivy Chen is a dedicated financial advisor specializing in Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) portfolio design. With a keen understanding of sustainable investment strategies, Ivy helps clients align their financial goals with their values, building portfolios that prioritize both profit and purpose. Her unique perspective stems from her interdisciplinary background, including her education at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts. Ivy holds the CSRIC accreditation, reflecting her commitment to responsible investing. She believes financial planning should be tailored to each individual's unique vision for the future. Beyond finance, Ivy's creative passion extends to filmmaking; she has directed three award-winning short films.
