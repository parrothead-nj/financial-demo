Farm Succession
Cornelius Husker is a seasoned financial advisor dedicated to securing the futures of farming families. A graduate of UNL, Cornelius specializes in farm succession planning, guiding clients through the complexities of transitioning their agricultural legacies to the next generation. He holds the AFIS accreditation, demonstrating his commitment to serving the unique needs of the agricultural community. Cornelius develops tailored strategies encompassing estate planning, retirement solutions, and risk management, ensuring a smooth and prosperous handover. He understands the deep connection farming families have to their land and works diligently to preserve their heritage. Outside of the office, Cornelius restores vintage tractors; he's especially proud of his fully functional 1948 model.
